What if I Want to Help More?

  1. Sign up for an ICIRR training: bit.ly/ICIRRKYR
  2. Consider volunteering with ICIRR as a courtwatcher: email icirrcourtwatch@gmail.com
  3. Consider signing up for a local school patrol - reach out to your local alderman and ask to be connected, or reach out to the principal at your child's school
  4. Wear a whistle and be ready to alert if you see ICE driving, and call 855-435-7693 and report to ICIRR after (use SALUTE)
  5. Consider putting up KYR flyers in your neighborhood, or sharing whistles and KYR zines in libraries, schools, churches and cafes near you
  6. Donate to OCAD's mutual aid fund for victims of ICE, and share their graphic below on social media: donorbox.org/mutualaid2025
  7. ...but most importantly

      8. Talk to as many neighbors, friends and community members about ICE and what they are doing!

      There may be people near you who need volunteers , but this work is often not shared on social media for obvious reasons. Get hyperlocal and find out who is already doing the work!
